Main message and mission statement
Our singular focus is on results. We review all published papers on the topic of prostate cancer, and if they meet our criteria, and include treatment results, we include them in our analysis. We then make these results readily available to patients, medical professionals and policy makers so they can make better decisions.

For patients

What should I do?

When faced with a diagnosis of prostate cancer, there are a lot of things to consider. Treatment methods, side-effects, recovery time, and what provider, But one question is primary: How do I achieve the best possible cancer control.
For doctors

Where does my experience fit in?

Seeing the results of the study in context can help inform treatment decisions, and provide patient direction. The foundation provides material that can be excerpted and included in peer presentations.
For policy makers

What policies will yield the best results?

For those that are responsible for allocating resources and seeking the best outcomes, the work of the Prostate Cancer Treatment Research Foundation can help provide clarity and focus.
